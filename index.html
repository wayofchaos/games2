THE FOUR TABLES

words · work · money · time
NIGHT SHIFTS
A writing game. Two novel campaigns, timed shifts, a refrain budget, and a wall of digits that grows as you do.
SLIPWAY
A project game. Ships with tides, crates on a crane that holds only three — finish before you start.
BRASS TACKS
A budget game. Envelopes, paydays, red ink that must be covered from somewhere — every dollar employed.
BIG ROCKS
A time game. A jar of fixed size, one spotlight, a mirror that learns how long your minutes really run.
Each game saves on this device, privately. Use Back up inside any game to move or keep your progress. Add this page to your Home Screen and the whole arcade becomes an app.
